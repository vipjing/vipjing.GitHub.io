Welcome to the TiDB Challenge Program Season Two!

This time, the Challenge follows the same rules but with more fun.

At present, most tasks show the most common requirements from the "My TiDB, My Call" voting activity. More tasks for usability improvement are coming on their way!

What tasks to complete? You have the final say!

HIGHLIGHTS

  • My TiDB, My call

    This challenge has opened up the voting activity for users to collect requirements. The more votes the requirement get, the more concerned the requirement is.

  • Easy to Hard

    If you are a newcomer to the challenge, you can get on board by completing easy tasks. Experienced players with more than 200 points can directly challenge medium and hard tasks!

  • Bountiful Prizes

    Season two comes back with more and better prizes! All contestants who have completed one or more task will be awarded Challenge Completed Badges. Your participation is highly appreciated!
